We want to keep Attorneys and Customers updated and informed with what is happening in the ‘World of Probate’. Probate is unique to Real Estate because of its detailed set of stringent laws that only effect Probate. In addition, these laws are different state by state, and just handling the additional red tape with this practice can become a second job. Here we have curated a selection of videos below that will help you understand your next steps in such a trying time. We also have a FAQ page where you can find answers to many of the questions that will help you be ready for any further questions you may need to consult with an attorney.

What is Probate?

First, we need to learn the
basics of Probate and what it is.

Probate is the legal process whereby a will is "proved" in a court and accepted as a valid public document that is the true last testament of the deceased.
